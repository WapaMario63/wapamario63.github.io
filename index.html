The Wapa Castle Team Minecraft Bukkit Plugins

About ##

Welcome, this is where we make all of our own bukkit plugins for our Minecraft server called WCTCraft. Not all plugins are available for anyone to use on their own servers till they are user friendly enough and that they are at least (if possible) open-beta or completed and out of beta, depending of how serious the plugin is.

FreakCraft (UNDER DEVELOPMENT)

About

This is a huge plugin. it adds a custom Boss system to the game with a variety of different bosses, from the so known Herobrine to some insane ones like Darth Vader.

One player will become the boss and play as him with special abilities, while, up to 30 players, fight it to death, each boss has different abilities, so does every player on each boss, each player has different equipment on each boss to help them be able to defeat the boss.
